Avocado Pesto Pasta

A vibrant and creamy pasta dish made with ripe avocados, fresh basil, garlic, and lemon juice blended into a luscious pesto sauce.

ğŸ§‚ Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados, peeled and pitted
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 300g cooked pasta (spaghetti or penne)
  • Cherry tomatoes and pine nuts for garnish

  1. In a blender, combine avocados, basil, garlic, lemon juice, olive oil, and Parmesan (if using). Blend until smooth.
  2. Season the pesto with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Toss the cooked pasta with the avocado pesto until evenly coated.
  4. Garnish with halved cherry tomatoes and toasted pine nuts.
  5. Serve immediately for best flavor and texture.
