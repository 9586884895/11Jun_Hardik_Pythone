  • Blue lagoon Mojito

    1 oz fresh lime juice, 1/2 to 1 oz blue curacao (liqueur or non-alcoholic syrup), 1 oz simple syrup (adjust to taste, or use 2 tsp sugar), 6-8 fresh mint leaves, plus a sprig for garnish, Club soda or sparkling water to top, Crushed ice, Lime wedge or wheel for garnish.

  • Deserts

    1 can sweetened condensed milk (14 ounces / ~400 grams), 2 cups heavy whipping cream, chilled 2 teaspoons vanilla extract, Large mixing bowl, Electric mixer (hand or stand), Freezer-safe container, Cling wrap or parchment paper.

  • Bread pakora

    Bread pakora also known as bread pakoda, are a popular North Indian breakfast & tea-time snack made with bread slices, potatoes, spices, gram flour and herbs. Bread pakora is not a common thing in Indian homes but are quite popular on the streets of India, mainly in Mumbai.
